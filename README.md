# Signals-and-Sequence
Construction and analysis of signals and sequences using MATLAB, covering time-domain operations, sampling theorem, Fourier series and transforms, convolution, correlation, and generation of various standard signals and sequences.

# Topics covered 

1. **Signal operations**
   Operations like addition, multiplication, scaling, shifting, and folding of signals and sequences.
   
2. **Generation of vaious signals and sequences**
   Includes impulse, step, square, sawtooth, sinusoidal, ramp, sinc, rectangular and signum signals/sequences.
      
3. **Even and Odd parts**
   Separating a signal into its even and odd components.
   
4. **Real and Imaginary parts**
   Analyzing real and imaginary components of complex signals.
   
5. **Sampling Theorem**
    Verifying the sampling theorem
    
6. **Convolution of Singals and Sequence**
    Implementation of linear convolution
   
7. **Autocorrelation and Cross-Correlation of Signals and Sequences**
   Finding signal similarity through auto- and cross-correlation analysis.
   
8. **Linearity and Time-Invariance**
    Testing systems for linearity and time-invariant properties.
   
9. **Unit sample, unit step and sinusoidal response**
    Analyzing unit sample, unit step, and sinusoidal responses of systems.
   
10. **Trigonometric and exponential Fourier series**
     Representation of periodic signals using Fourier series.
    
12. **Fourier Transform of a signal**
    Frequency-domain analysis using the Fourier transform.
